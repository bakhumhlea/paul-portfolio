About Project
Zupply is a food supply ordering mobile application which connects chefs and restaurant owners to their supplier, provides ordering system, newsletter feeding, and instant messages services right to your mobile device.
Create Workflow
Instead of writing an email or leave a voice mail to suppliers when you need to order fish or veggies for delivering the next days or browsing through your email inbox to find order history, Zupply reduces all those complicate tasks into a few easy steps and create a simple workflow which agile and well-organized.
