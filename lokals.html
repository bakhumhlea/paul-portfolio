About project
A social-advertising platform for small businesses such as restaurant, coffee shop, boutique to handicraft store, aims to be a place for businesses to connect with their customers, through its activities.

Lokals provide service to both businesses and end-users. The business can create its profile, write posts, schedule events, and publish them on the platform where the end-users can browse through, exploring the new experiences nearby.
Newsfeed
The result on the Lokals's newsfeed based on the posts which could be simple text posts or event/activities advertises. End-users can scroll through the page to find anything they like within the scope that match between user preference and the tag keywords embed on each post and activities.
API and Map
Lokals prototype used a few APIs from the third party; it used Google Geolocation and Google Places for searching businesses and matched the results with the posts and activities record in its database, then return them to the end-user. Its also used Mapbox as its main map engine to display the results when the user finds something base on location or visually pick from the map.
